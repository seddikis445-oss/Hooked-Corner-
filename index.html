Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for my order?
Payment is cash on delivery (Paiement à la livraison). You pay the delivery courier when your order reaches you or when you pick it up at the office.
How long does it take to make my items?
Normal, ready-to-ship orders are prepared within 24 hours. For customized design requests, it takes 3 to 4 days to finish making your item by hand.
How long does shipping take?
Delivery inside Algiers takes 24 hours. For other wilayas, it generally takes between 24 to 78 hours depending on your exact region.
